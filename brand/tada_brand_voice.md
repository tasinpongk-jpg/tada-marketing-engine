# TADA Thailand — Brand Voice Guide

## Who we are
TADA is the zero-commission ride-hailing platform, launched in Bangkok
February 2024. Drivers keep 100% of the fare — we charge only the
government-regulated THB 20 booking fee. ~15,000 registered drivers
(partnership with Howa International). Backed by MVLLABS Group.

## Core narrative (every piece of content ladders up to one of these)
1. **Fairness** — 0% commission vs Grab's ~25–30% and LINE MAN's ~10%.
   Drivers earn more, so service is better.
2. **Driver welfare** — happy drivers, better rides. Tell driver stories.
3. **Challenger spirit** — we can't outspend Grab; we out-care them.
4. **Transparency** — flat THB 20 fee, no hidden cuts, no surge games.

## Tone
- Trustworthy, modern, slightly playful.
- Thai copy: casual friendly ภาษาพูด — never formal/bureaucratic Thai.
  Use cultural hooks Thai audiences respond to: รถติด jokes, sanook (สนุก)
  energy, real driver/rider testimonial voice.
- English copy: confident, warm, plain words. No corporate jargon.

## Audiences
| Segment | Angle | Platforms |
|---|---|---|
| Bangkok commuters 25–40 | Better service because drivers are fairly paid | Facebook, Google UAC |
| Younger riders 18–28 | Fun, authentic, driver-POV content | TikTok, IG Reels |
| Drivers (recruit/retain) | Keep 100% of your fare — quantify vs Grab/LINE MAN | Facebook groups, LINE OA |
| Existing riders | Retention, coupons, weekend pushes | LINE OA broadcast |

## Rules
- Never claim market leadership; we are the challenger.
- Always quantify the commission gap when targeting drivers
  (e.g. on THB 30,000/month gross, Grab takes ~THB 7,500–9,000; TADA takes 0).
- Every LINE broadcast: ≤3 lines, 1 emoji minimum, strong CTA to open app.
- Facebook ads: ≤80 words, hook in first line.
- Never invent statistics or savings percentages. The only numbers allowed
  in copy without a cited source: THB 20 flat fee, 0% commission, competitor
  commission ranges from the watchlist (Grab ~25–30%, LINE MAN ~10%).
- All AI-generated copy is a DRAFT — human review before posting, always.
  LINE broadcasts must also pass LINE Thailand messaging-policy review.
