# Content Pillars & Platform Playbook — Tada Thailand

## Platform priorities
| Platform | Use case | Content type | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Facebook | Brand awareness, app installs | Video ads, carousel promos, driver stories | 4–5 posts/wk |
| TikTok | Riders 18–28 | Driver POV, day-in-the-life, meme-driven | 3–4/wk |
| LINE OA | Rider retention, coupons | Broadcasts, chatbot promo redemptions | 1–2 broadcasts/wk |
| Instagram | Visual brand | City/lifestyle photography, Reels | 3/wk |
| Google UAC | App installs | Performance Max, keyword targeting | always-on |

## Content pillars (weekly mix)
1. **Driver fairness** (30%) — 0% commission stories, earnings comparisons,
   driver spotlights.
2. **Rider value** (25%) — promos, weekend pushes, flat-fee transparency.
3. **Sanook/culture** (25%) — รถติด humor, Bangkok life, trends, memes
   (GVN's meme-led creative drove +253% app installs — lean in).
4. **Trust/transparency** (20%) — how the THB 20 fee works, safety,
   behind-the-scenes.

## Calendar inputs
- Thai holidays & moments: Songkran (Apr), Loy Krathong (Nov), long weekends,
  rainy-season traffic peaks (May–Oct), back-to-school, year-end parties.
- Counter-programming: when Grab/LINE MAN run a big promo, answer with a
  fairness/value narrative within 48h — never a discount war.
